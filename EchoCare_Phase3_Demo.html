📖 Guided Walkthrough Instructions
  1. Step 1: Invite Papa to a Group — On Sandeep's phone (left), navigate to Groups → Tap Create Family Group → Tap Create Group & Invite. Watch the invitation sync to the Cloud!
  2. Step 2: Accept invitation on Papa's device — Tapping Create Group on Sandeep's phone sends the invite. On Papa's phone (right), notice the red badge on the top right, tap the invite icon, and click Accept on the invitation card. Watch it sync back to Sandeep's screen.
  3. Step 3: Create a cross-device voice reminder — On Sandeep's phone (left), tap the Home icon → Click the Microphone FAB (purple mic icon) → click the first simulation command: "Tell Papa to take his medicine at 8 PM every day". Tap Confirm on the Confirmation screen.
  4. Step 4: Watch the FCM push delivery sync — On clicking confirm, watch the reminder sync packet animate from Sandeep's phone to the Cloud icon, and watch the FCM push notification dispatch directly to Papa's phone! The BP Medication reminder card will instantly slide onto Papa's Home screen dashboard.
  5. Step 5: Trigger alarm & listen to TTS — On Papa's phone, hover over the BP Medication reminder card and tap **"🔔 Firing Test"** to trigger the lock screen. Listen to the voice synthesize: "Papa, please take your medicine". Tap **Acknowledge** to stop it, which updates Sandeep's screen status to Acknowledged!
